📱 Recording on Mobile

Mobile browsers can't record video directly. Here's the easiest workaround:

Step 1 — Start Screen Recorder iPhone: Swipe down from top-right → tap ⏺ Screen Record
Android: Swipe down → Screen Recorder tile
Step 2 — Tap "Start with Camera" Your script + your face will both appear on screen
Step 3 — Hit Play in the app The script scrolls, your face is recorded via screen recorder
Step 4 — Stop screen recorder when done Video saves to your Photos / Gallery automatically
